Privacy Policy

How we handle information from this website.

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Tuff Bros Mobile Detailing respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through tuffbrosdetailing.com, why we collect it, and the choices available to you.

Information you provide

When you request a quote, you may provide your name, phone number, email address, city, vehicle details, requested service, vehicle condition, and message. We use this information to respond to your request, prepare a quote, schedule service, and provide customer support.

Website analytics and cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Analytics may collect information such as pages visited, approximate city or region, device and browser type, referring source, and interactions with site features such as calls, texts, and quote requests.

Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to provide these reports. We do not intentionally send the contents of quote-form fields, such as your name, phone number, or email address, to Google Analytics. You can learn more about how Google uses cookies.

How we share information

We use service providers to operate this website and respond to quote requests, including Formspree for form processing and Google Analytics for site measurement. We may also disclose information if required to comply with law, protect our rights, or help prevent fraud or abuse. We do not sell personal information.

Your choices

You may choose not to submit a quote form and may contact us by phone instead. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You can also use the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on to prevent Analytics measurement in supported browsers.

Data security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information submitted through our site and retain it only as long as needed for customer service, business operations, or legal obligations. No method of online transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact us

For questions about this policy or your information, call or text (501) 500-4306, or use our quote request form.
