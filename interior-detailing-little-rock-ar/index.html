A Deep Clean, Not a Quick Vacuum

Interior detailing goes far beyond running a vacuum over the floor mats. We work through the cabin piece by piece:

  • Seats and upholstery — cleaned and conditioned
  • Carpets and floor mats — deep cleaned
  • Dash, console, and door panels — detailed and dressed
  • Cupholders, vents, seams, and crevices
  • Interior glass, cleaned streak-free

Pet Hair, Stains & Odors

Pet hair is one of the most requested (and most stubborn) interior problems. We use specialized tools to lift embedded hair out of carpet and upholstery instead of just chasing it around with a vacuum.

Honest expectations: most stains and odors improve dramatically, and many disappear completely. Some — old dye transfer, bleach spots, or stains that have set for years — can be permanent. We'll always tell you what's realistic before we start, and our odor treatment targets the source rather than masking it with fragrance.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you remove all stains?

Most stains improve dramatically and many come out completely, but some — old dye transfer, bleach damage, or long-set stains — can be permanent. We'll give you an honest read before we start.

Is pet hair removal extra?

Heavy pet hair takes real time and specialized tools, so it's priced as an add-on based on how much there is. Mention it (or show it in your photos) when you request a quote.

How long does an interior detail take?

A quick interior clean takes about an hour. Deep cleans on heavily soiled or pet-hair vehicles can take 2–3 hours.

Do you clean around car seats?

Yes — we'll clean around and under child seats. For safety, we ask that you remove and reinstall the car seats themselves.

Do I need to provide water or power?

No. We bring our own water and power to every appointment.
