Full Detail vs. Basic In & Out

Our Basic In & Out (from $150) is a solid maintenance clean — wash, vacuum, wipe-down. A Full Detail Package is a different level: every surface inside and out gets individual attention, which is why it takes 2–4 hours instead of one.

If your vehicle hasn't been professionally detailed in over a year — or ever — the full detail is almost always the right starting point.

What's Included

Interior:

  • Deep-cleaned seats, carpets, and mats
  • Dash, console, panels, cupholders, and vents detailed
  • Streak-free interior glass

Exterior:

  • Careful hand wash and dry
  • Wax for shine and protection
  • Wheels, tires, and trim detailed and dressed

Best For

Full details are our most popular package for a reason. They're ideal for:

  • Neglected vehicles that need a true reset
  • Resale prep — a detailed car photographs better and sells for more
  • Family vehicles collecting crumbs, spills, and pet hair
  • Daily drivers that deserve a fresh start

Pricing starts at $300 for cars and $350 for SUVs and trucks, with the final number based on size and condition. Considering long-term protection? Ask about adding ceramic coating after your detail.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full detail take?

Typically 2–4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. Heavily soiled vehicles can take longer — we don't rush the job.

What's the difference between a full detail and the Basic In & Out?

The Basic In & Out is a maintenance-level wash, vacuum, and wipe-down. The full detail deep cleans every surface inside and out, including seats, carpets, and paint protection.

Do I need to be home the whole time?

No — as long as we have access to the vehicle and a safe place to work, you can go about your day.

How much does a full detail cost?

From $300 for cars and $350 for SUVs and trucks. Condition matters, so text photos for an exact quote.

Is engine bay cleaning included?

Engine bay cleaning is available as an add-on — just ask when you request your quote.
