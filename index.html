Frequently Asked Questions

Do you come to me?

Yes. We're a fully mobile detailing service — we bring our equipment and water supply directly to your home, office, or job site anywhere in our service area.

How much does detailing cost?

Prices start at $75 and vary based on vehicle size, condition, and services selected. See our pricing section above, or request a free quote for an exact price.

How long does a full detail take?

A full detail typically takes 2–4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

Can you remove stains?

In most cases, yes. We treat common stains, spills, and odors as part of our interior detailing and odor treatment services.

Do you offer ceramic coating?

Yes. Ceramic coating is quoted individually based on your vehicle's size and current paint condition. Request a free quote to get started.

Can I send photos for a quote?

Absolutely — photos help us give you the most accurate price. Mention it in your quote request and we'll follow up on how to send them.
