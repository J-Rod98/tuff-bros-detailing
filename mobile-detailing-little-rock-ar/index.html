What Mobile Detailing Includes

Mobile detailing means the full detail shop comes to your driveway. Every appointment is done on-site with our own water supply, power, and professional equipment — you don't need to provide anything but a place to park.

Depending on the package you choose, mobile detailing can include:

  • Exterior hand wash, wax, and wheel & tire cleaning
  • Interior vacuuming, wipe-down, and deep cleaning
  • Full inside-and-out detail packages
  • Add-ons like pet hair removal, odor treatment, and headlight restoration

Why Mobile Beats the Drive-Through Wash

A tunnel wash is fast, but the spinning brushes grind yesterday's grit into your paint and leave the interior untouched. Mobile detailing is hand work: careful washing that protects your clear coat, and interior cleaning a machine simply can't do.

You also skip the errand entirely. Instead of sitting in a lobby, your car gets detailed while you work from home, take a meeting, or spend the afternoon with your family.

Who It's For

Most of our customers are busy people in Little Rock and North Little Rock who want a clean vehicle without giving up half a day: families with kid-and-pet messes, professionals who meet clients, folks prepping a car to sell, and local businesses keeping work vehicles sharp.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you come to me?

Yes — that's the whole point. We detail at your home, office, or job site anywhere in our Little Rock-area service zone.

Do you bring your own water and power?

Yes. We arrive fully self-contained with our own water supply and power, so you don't need to provide hookups.

How long does mobile detailing take?

A basic wash and wax usually takes about an hour; a full detail runs 2–4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

How much does it cost?

Starting prices range from $75 for a basic wash and wax to $300+ for a full detail. Text photos of your vehicle for the most accurate quote.

What do you need from me?

Just a driveway, parking spot, or workspace where we're allowed to set up. We handle the rest.

What if it rains?

Arkansas weather happens. If conditions won't allow quality work, we'll reach out and reschedule at no charge.
